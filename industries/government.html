"Grievances sit unacknowledged for 30 days. By then, they have escalated to the minister's office."
The Cost
Unresolved grievances erode public trust and create political risk.
What We Build For You

✓
Every grievance acknowledged within 1 hour
✓
Routing accuracy near 100%, reducing misdirected complaints

SLA breaches surfaced to senior officers before escalation

Agent Specialities
Your AI Workforce for Government & Public Sector
Agent 01

IAS Exam Prep

Evaluates mains answers against UPSC standards, identifies weak areas in specific subjects, creates personalised study plans, delivers daily current affairs briefings, and conducts mock interviews. providing the structured feedback that separates success from repetition.

Agent 02

Citizen Grievance

Receives complaints across WhatsApp, web, and IVR, categorises grievances against complaint codes, routes to appropriate departments, generates tracking IDs for citizens, and escalates SLA breaches. ensuring no complaint disappears.

Agent 03

Policy Research

Scans government circulars, policy documents, and regulatory changes, summarises impact on your department's operations, maps compliance requirements, and flags policy windows before they close. keeping your team policy-aware, not policy-blindsided.

Agent 04

RTI Processing

Triages RTI requests by category and complexity, researches available data and department records, drafts structured responses in statutory format, tracks all statutory deadlines, and alerts officers well before deadline. ensuring zero RTI defaults.

Agent 05

Scheme Eligibility

Evaluates citizen applications against scheme eligibility criteria, identifies missing documents, guides applicants through the process, generates eligibility summaries for approvers, and maintains audit trails. ensuring fair and transparent administration.
