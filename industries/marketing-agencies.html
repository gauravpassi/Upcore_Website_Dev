✓

✓
Revenue per sales rep increases without adding headcount
Agent Specialities
Your AI Workforce for Marketing & Agencies
Agent 01

Content Creator

Produces blog posts, social content, and ad copy trained on your brand voice and guidelines, maintains consistent tone across channels, optimises for SEO and engagement. enabling 5-10x content output without sacrificing quality or brand consistency.

Agent 02

SEO Research

Identifies high-intent keywords in your niche, analyzes competitor content and rankings, surfaces content gaps and opportunities, generates topic ideas aligned with search intent. accelerating SEO strategy development and content prioritization.

Agent 03

Campaign Analytics

Pulls performance data from ad platforms and analytics tools, calculates KPIs and trends, identifies top and bottom performers, surfaces actionable optimisation opportunities. enabling faster, data-driven campaign adjustments.

Agent 04

Client Reporting

Aggregates performance data, writes narrative commentary on results and trends, formats reports in your agency template, schedules report generation. cutting report creation from hours to minutes and freeing AMs for relationship building.

Agent 05

Lead Scoring

Enriches leads with firmographic and behavioural data, scores by ICP fit and intent signals, routes top prospects to sales with context summaries, tracks conversion by segment. improving sales efficiency and revenue per rep without adding headcount.
